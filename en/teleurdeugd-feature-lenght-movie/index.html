Teleurdeugd [Feature lenght movie]

[  Directing, 2nd Camera, Audio recording, audio postproduction, editing, effects and animation  ]  ||
Teleurdeugd speaks about the omnipresent theme of depression and psychological issues without trying to moralize the audience. The film doesn’t offer answers or solutions, instead it invites to reflect on and speak about these problems. It had a theatrical release with 800 sold tickets.
